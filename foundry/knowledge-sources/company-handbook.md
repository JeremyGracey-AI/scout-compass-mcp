## Offices and hours

- **Headquarters:** 500 Harbor Blvd, San Mateo, CA. Open 8:00–18:00 PT.
- **Austin hub:** 1100 Congress Ave, Austin, TX. Open 9:00–18:00 CT.
- **Remote:** most engineering and design roles are remote-first within the US.
- Building access uses badge entry. Lost badges are reissued by Workplace Ops at the front desk.

## Time off

- Salaried employees accrue paid time off at 1.67 days per month (20 days/year).
- PTO requests go through the HR tool (Bamboo) and need manager approval at least 3 business days ahead for blocks longer than one week.
- 11 company holidays per year, published each January in the shared calendar.
- Sick leave is separate from PTO and is not capped for full-time staff.

## IT onboarding

- New hires receive a laptop (MacBook Pro 14" default; Linux ThinkPad on request) shipped to arrive before the start date.
- Accounts are provisioned through SSO (Okta). Day-one access: email, chat, the engineering wiki, and the HR tool.
- Hardware issues go to the IT help desk via the #it-help channel; loaner laptops are available same-day at HQ.
- Password resets and MFA enrollment are self-service in Okta.

## Equipment and expenses

- Home-office stipend: a one-time $750 allowance for desk, chair, and peripherals.
- Reimbursable purchases are submitted with a receipt in the expense tool within 30 days.
- Standard software licenses (IDE, design tools) are pre-approved; anything else needs manager sign-off.

## Travel

- Book flights and hotels through the corporate travel tool (TravelPerk).
- Domestic economy and standard hotel tiers are pre-approved; upgrades are out of pocket.
- Mileage for personal-car business travel is reimbursed at the current IRS rate.

## Code of conduct

- Treat colleagues, customers, and partners with respect.
- Report security concerns to security@northwind-robotics.example immediately.
- Conflicts of interest are disclosed to your manager and People Ops.
